DAO Treasury Management

Democratize decision-making with our decentralized autonomous organization treasury platform. Create proposals, vote collectively, and execute financial decisions with complete transparency.

Perfect for communities, organizations, and groups that want to manage funds democratically without central authority.

  • Proposal-based governance system
  • Member voting with wallet signatures
  • Quorum-based fund releases
  • Transparent transaction history
  • Multi-signature security

ROSCA Savings Circles

Reimagine traditional rotating savings and credit associations (ROSCA) for the blockchain era. Create trusted savings groups where members contribute regularly and receive payouts in turns.

Known as "Esusu" in many cultures, this time-tested method of community finance is now automated, transparent, and trustless on Cardano.

  • Automated contribution tracking
  • Scheduled payout rotation
  • Smart contract enforcement
  • No intermediaries needed
  • Complete transparency for all members
