ROSCA Savings Circles

Savings Community Rotation

What is ROSCA?

ROSCA (Rotating Savings and Credit Association) is a traditional community savings method where members contribute fixed amounts regularly, and each member receives the full pot in turns according to a predetermined order.

üåç Cultural Context

ROSCAs are known by different names globally:

  • Esusu - Nigeria and West Africa
  • Stokvel - South Africa
  • Tandas - Mexico and Latin America
  • Hui - China
  • Chit Fund - India

Our platform digitizes this centuries-old practice with blockchain security.

How ROSCA Works

A simple example with 6 members contributing 10 ADA per month:

ROSCA Rotation: Each member receives 60 ADA when their turn comes

Key Concepts

Deposit Amount

The fixed amount each member contributes per round (e.g., 10 ADA).

Participants

List of wallet addresses participating in the circle. Order matters for payout sequence.

Payout Order

The sequence in which members receive their turn (0, 1, 2, 3...). This is set at creation and cannot be changed.

Round

A complete cycle where one member receives a payout. If there are 6 members, the circle completes after 6 rounds.

ROSCA Circle Architecture

ROSCA Timeline: Automated rotation ensures fair distribution

System Flow

  1. Circle Creation: Organizer sets deposit amount, participants, and payout order
  2. Deposits: All members deposit their contribution
  3. Smart Contract Lock: Funds held securely until payout conditions met
  4. Automated Payout: System releases funds to current beneficiary
  5. Rotation: Next member becomes beneficiary for following round
  6. Completion: Circle concludes after all members receive payout

Security Best Practices

üîí Critical Security Rules
  • Never share your seed phrase - Not with support, not with members, not with anyone
  • Verify all addresses - Always double-check wallet addresses before sending funds
  • Use hardware wallets - For large amounts, consider Ledger or Trezor
  • Check transaction details - Review amounts and recipients before signing
  • Beware of phishing - Only use official platform URLs

Smart Contract Security

Our platform uses audited smart contracts with the following security features:

  • Immutable Logic: Code cannot be changed after deployment
  • Multi-Signature Protection: DAO requires multiple approvals
  • Time Locks: ROSCA enforces rotation schedules
  • Access Control: Only authorized wallets can perform actions
  • Emergency Pause: Critical bug protection (requires governance vote)

Wallet Security Tips

Regularly update your wallet software and browser to patch security vulnerabilities.

Troubleshooting

Wallet Won't Connect

Problem: Connection popup doesn't appear or fails.

Solutions:

  • Refresh the page and try again
  • Check if your wallet extension is enabled
  • Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Brave)
  • Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Ensure wallet is unlocked and on Cardano mainnet

Transaction Pending Too Long

Problem: Transaction submitted but not confirming.

Solutions:

  • Wait 5-10 minutes for network congestion to clear
  • Check Cardano network status at cardanoscan.io
  • Verify transaction hash in blockchain explorer
  • If stuck after 30 minutes, contact support with transaction hash

Insufficient Funds Error

Problem: Transaction fails with "insufficient funds" message.

Solutions:

  • Ensure you have enough ADA for both the action AND transaction fees (~0.5 ADA extra)
  • Check your wallet balance
  • Wait for any pending transactions to complete
  • Try reducing the amount slightly

Can't Find My Circle/Proposal

Problem: Created circle or proposal doesn't appear.

Solutions:

  • Ensure transaction was confirmed (check wallet history)
  • Refresh the page
  • Verify you're connected with the correct wallet
  • Check if you're on the right platform (DAO vs ROSCA)
  • Wait a few minutes for blockchain indexing

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: What is Cardano?

A: Cardano is a blockchain platform that enables smart contracts and decentralized applications. It's known for low fees, fast transactions, and strong security through peer-reviewed research.

Q: Do I need technical knowledge to use the platform?

A: No. While blockchain concepts help, our interface is designed for non-technical users. Just follow the step-by-step guides in this documentation.

Q: How much does it cost to use?

A: The platform itself is free. You only pay Cardano network transaction fees (typically 0.17-0.5 ADA per transaction). There are no platform fees or subscriptions.

Q: Is my money safe?

A: Yes. Funds are secured by smart contracts on the Cardano blockchain. No individual (including us) can access or control your funds. Only the pre-programmed rules can move funds.

DAO Questions

Q: Can I change a proposal after submitting?

A: No. Proposals are immutable once on-chain. Always double-check details before submitting.

Q: What happens if we don't reach quorum?

A: The proposal remains open indefinitely until quorum is reached. Funds stay locked in the treasury and no execution can occur.

Q: Can I vote multiple times?

A: No. Each wallet address can vote only once per proposal. Votes cannot be changed.

Q: Who can trigger execution?

A: Any member can trigger execution once quorum is met. The smart contract verifies quorum automatically.

ROSCA Questions

Q: What if someone doesn't deposit?

A: The smart contract enforces deposits. If a member fails to deposit, the circle cannot progress. Choose trustworthy participants.

Q: Can I leave a circle early?

A: No. Joining a ROSCA is a binding commitment. Leaving disrupts all other members. Only join circles you can complete.

Q: Can the payout order be changed?

A: No. The payout order is set at creation and enforced by the smart contract. It cannot be altered.

Q: What happens if I receive my payout early?

A: You're still required to deposit for all remaining rounds. The circle isn't complete until all members have received their payout.
